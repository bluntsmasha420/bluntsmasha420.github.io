Testimonials

our customer reviews

  • Had the island tacos at summer in the city. So so amazing. The homemade flat bread “shell” was so soft and yet held up to the delicious toppings. It was so so flavourful, with the perfect amount of heat while not burning your mouth off so you could taste all the various components, the juicy pork and sweet mango salsa. Great recommendation from the guys working the truck!

  • Phenomenal Carribean food. I ordered the poutine, which was a perfect balance of flavours. The Tamarind added a unique sweet and sour touch and cut into the habanero heat, and the Aioli brought a freshness to the perfectly spiced chickpeas. I then went back and ordered some doubles. Thank you for a journey to Trinidad right here in Winnipeg.
